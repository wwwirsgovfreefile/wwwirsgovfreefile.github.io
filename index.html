Eligible taxpayers can freely access online tax preparation & e-filing software via www.irs.gov/freefile. The online tax preparation software is used for accessing online. Get professional-quality tax preparation services at IRS portal for free. No need to pay for the services.


How to fill out IRS Free File?

File your taxes online for free -

  1. For filling out tax forms through IRS Free File feature.
  2. Gather the necessary documents like W-2 forms, 1099 forms, Last year’s tax return, Social Security numbers & Bank account information.
  3. Now, visit official IRS Free File portal - www.irs.gov/freefile.
  4. Thereafter, select Free File Fillable Forms option.
  5. Enter basic information - Your name, Date of birth, Address, Social Security number (SSN).
  6. Type Tax information including Personal, Income, Deductions and Credits.
  7. Further, review & submit your return.
  8. IRS notifies within 24-48 hours of successful submission.
